About the Legacy Fund

The Legacy Fund exists to quietly support classmates who may need a little help—whether it's covering reunion costs, sending flowers during illness, or offering gestures of care in times of loss. It's a way for our class to take care of each other. Contributions are optional and deeply appreciated.

Please note the classmate's name in the check memo line (if it's not obvious from the check).
